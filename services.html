Our Services

Woodleaf Baptist Church offers worship service at 11:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m. on Sundays. The service offers singing of praise and expositional preaching/teaching from the Bible delivered in a simple, easy to understand fashion.

We also have a wonderful nursery service for ages 0 to 24 months during the Sunday morning worship, giving parents the opportunity to worship without having to worry about their small children. Kids age 2 through 6 can attend our Children's Church, led by Lisa Robertson. It is a wonderful way for children to experience God's word! Our wonderful teachers go over lessons from the bible on a level that they can understand. They learn songs and do activities that make learning about Jesus fun and exciting.

Past worship services are available for viewing an our Watch Online page.

Jesus Squad

The Jr. Youth Ministry of WBC, Jesus Squad, exists to bring young people into age appropriate ministry, where they are shown how to develop a deeper relationship with Christ and encouraged to build uplifting relationships with others.

Teens

Our Teen Group focuses on the day to day struggles teenagers go through in school and daily life. We discuss how they can strengthen and use their faith when combating struggles and how it can comfort as well.

Adult Bible Study

Wednesday evening Bible study is lead by Pastor Chris, with an open discussion type service where anyone can ask questions or share their opinions.

Men's Group

First Saturday of Every Month

Once a month the men get together to have a breakfast and fellowship together. Following breakfast we have a time of devotion together in God's Holy word. We encourage all men to come out. We also are looking to start moving throughout the community to invite new people to church and win souls for Christ. Another thing our group does is if anyone is in need of help with moving, grass cutting, ramp building (handicap), etc. we try to go and help.
